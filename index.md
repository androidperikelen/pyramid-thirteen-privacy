# Privacy Policy for Pyramid Thirteen

**Last updated: 27 April 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Pyramid Thirteen ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information collected automatically

When you use Pyramid Thirteen, the following may be collected:

- **Device information**: Device model, operating system version, app version, language, and region settings.
- **Usage data**: Game progress, statistics, and session information stored locally on your device.
- **Advertising identifiers**: Google Advertising ID (AAID) collected by our advertising provider for serving personalized advertisements.

### Information stored locally on your device

Pyramid Thirteen stores the following on your device only and does not send it to us:

- High scores and game statistics
- Daily challenge progress and streaks
- User preferences (sound, music, difficulty)

This data is stored in your device's local storage and is removed if you uninstall the app.

## Third-Party Services

Pyramid Thirteen uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data such as your device's advertising ID, IP address, general location (country/region), and information about your interactions with ads. For more information, please review:

- [Google AdMob Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings:
- **Android**: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Children's Privacy

Pyramid Thirteen is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

We comply with the Google Play Families Policy and AdMob's child-directed treatment requirements where applicable.

## Data Retention

- Data stored locally on your device persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data.
- AdMob data retention is governed by Google's policies (typically up to 14 months for advertising data).

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access the data stored locally by clearing the app cache or uninstalling
- Opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings
- Request information about data collected by Google AdMob through their privacy controls

## Security

Game data is stored locally on your device using standard mobile storage mechanisms. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
